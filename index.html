The Avatar Cycle

Water Earth Fire Air is a version of Rock Paper Scissors in the Avatar The Last Airbender Universe.

It's made after the Avatar Cycle, which is the order in which the Avatars are born into one of the Four Nations (Water Earth Fire or Air Nation). The cycle is also the order in which each Avatar is supposed to learn these four elements.

The picture above illustrates it well (Earth Nation Avatar > Fire Nation Avatar > Air Nation Avatar > Water Nation Avatar > Earth Nation Avatar again and so on...)

Rules

The rules to determine which element wins are based on the avatar cycle, so:

Water beats Earth

Earth beats Fire

Fire beats Air

Air beats Water

There are also two types of ties:

When the elements are the same

When the elements are opposite

For example: Water&Fire and Earth&Air
